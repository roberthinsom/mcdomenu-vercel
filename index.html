McDonald’s Menu Philippines 2024: From Breakfast Delights to Happy Meals

Breakfast Delights: A New Morning Tradition

Savory Morning Treats

The day starts off right with McDonald’s 2024 breakfast offerings. This year’s menu highlights a blend of traditional favorites and innovative options. The McChicken Muffin, featuring a crispy chicken patty with a hint of savory sausage, is a new twist on the classic McMuffin. The Spicy Longganisa McMuffin brings a local flair to your breakfast, combining the beloved Filipino longganisa sausage with a perfectly toasted muffin.

For those who enjoy a hearty breakfast, the Big Breakfast Deluxe now includes a new addition: a rich, creamy mushroom gravy that pairs perfectly with the fluffy scrambled eggs and crispy hash browns.

Sweet Morning Choices

Sweet tooths aren’t left out with the introduction of the Mango Peach Parfait, a delicious blend of ripe mangoes, peaches, and a creamy yogurt base, topped with crunchy granola. It’s a refreshing way to start the day and a perfect option for those seeking a lighter breakfast.

Lunch and Dinner: A Feast for Every Palate

Burger Innovations

McDonald’s continues to impress with its burger lineup. The Bacon Cheddar McRoyale, featuring a juicy beef patty topped with smoky bacon, cheddar cheese, and a tangy barbecue sauce, is a must-try for burger enthusiasts. For a more local twist, the Adobo Burger incorporates the rich flavors of Filipino adobo, with tender, marinated beef served on a soft bun with pickled onions and garlic aioli.

Global Flavors, Local Touches

The Korean BBQ Chicken Sandwich is another standout, combining spicy Korean BBQ sauce with a crispy chicken fillet. Meanwhile, the Sinigang Fries offer a unique blend of traditional Filipino sinigang flavor with a crunchy French fry base, a perfect example of McDonald’s fusion of global and local tastes.

Happy Meals: Fun and Flavor for Kids

Classic Favorites

The Happy Meal continues to be a favorite among children, offering a well-balanced meal with a choice of a burger or chicken nuggets, paired with apple slices or fries, and a drink. This year, McDonald’s has introduced Superhero-Themed Happy Meals featuring special packaging and collectible toys inspired by popular characters from local and international superhero franchises.

New Kid-Friendly Options

In response to growing dietary preferences, McDonald’s has also added a Healthy Happy Meal option. This includes a smaller portion of chicken nuggets, a side of fruit or carrot sticks, and a choice of milk or water. It’s a great way to offer kids a nutritious alternative without compromising on taste.

Dessert and Beverages: Sweet Endings and Refreshing Sips

Delectable Desserts

No meal is complete without a sweet treat. The Ube McFlurry, a delightful twist on a classic favorite, combines creamy vanilla soft serve with the vibrant flavor of ube, a popular Filipino purple yam. Additionally, the Choco-Coffee Sundae offers a rich blend of coffee-flavored ice cream topped with chocolate syrup for those who love a coffee kick with their dessert.

Refreshing Beverages

For drinks, the Tropical Citrus Iced Tea is a refreshing option with hints of pineapple and orange, perfect for cooling down on a hot day. The Matcha Green Tea Shake is also new this year, offering a unique and delicious blend of creamy vanilla shake with matcha’s distinct flavor.
